The Philosophy

Sound is the invisible architecture of memory. My practice focuses on the intersection of cinematic storytelling and acoustic ecology, creating immersive environments that breathe life into digital and physical spaces.

With a background in classical composition and experimental field recording, I approach every project as a unique sonic landscape waiting to be discovered. Based in Tokyo / Working Globally.
